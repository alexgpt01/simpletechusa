Project-Based Technology Consulting

When you need expert guidance for a specific technology initiative, Simple.Tech provides senior-level consulting with clearly defined scope and deliverables. Whether you're planning a cloud migration, conducting a security audit, or designing new infrastructure, our consultants bring deep technical expertise and business acumen to every engagement.

Unlike traditional consulting firms that staff projects with junior analysts, every Simple.Tech consulting engagement is led by senior practitioners with 10+ years of hands-on experience. We deliver actionable recommendations grounded in real-world implementation expertise.

Comprehensive security evaluations including vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, policy reviews, and risk analysis. We identify security gaps and provide prioritized remediation roadmaps.

Cloud Migration Planning

Strategic planning for moving applications and infrastructure to cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud. We assess workloads, design migration strategies, and manage the transition process.

Infrastructure Design

Architecture and design services for network infrastructure, data centers, hybrid cloud environments, and enterprise systems. We create scalable, secure infrastructure that supports business growth.

Technology Roadmaps
