Technical Support

Senior-level technical support for computer issues, troubleshooting, and IT emergencies. Direct access to experienced engineers who resolve problems efficiently.

Expert Technical Support When You Need It

Simple.Tech provides technical support for businesses that need expert help without committing to full managed services. Whether you're dealing with a critical issue, need help with a specific technical challenge, or require temporary support during transitions, our senior engineers deliver efficient, professional assistance.

Unlike traditional helpdesk services that route you through multiple tiers of junior technicians, you work directly with experienced engineers who have the expertise to diagnose and resolve complex issues on first contact.

The Simple.Tech Difference

When you contact us for technical support, you speak with a senior engineer—not a Level 1 technician reading from a script. Our team has 10+ years of experience each, which means faster diagnosis, more accurate solutions, and less downtime for your business.

We focus on solving the actual problem, not just addressing symptoms. This approach costs you less and prevents recurring issues.

Technical Support Services

Computer & Laptop Support

Hardware diagnostics, software troubleshooting, performance optimization, and repair coordination for desktops, laptops, and workstations.

Malware & Security Issues

Virus removal, malware cleanup, ransomware response, and security incident investigation. We identify how infections occurred and prevent recurrence.

Network Troubleshooting

Connectivity issues, network performance problems, Wi-Fi troubleshooting, and configuration assistance for routers, switches, and access points.

Software Installation & Configuration

Application installation, software configuration, licensing issues, and compatibility troubleshooting for business software and productivity tools.

Email & Communication Issues

Email problems, Microsoft 365 troubleshooting, calendar and contact sync issues, and communication platform configuration.

Emergency Support

Critical issue response for business-impacting problems that require immediate attention. Available for urgent situations outside of standard business hours.

Data Recovery Assistance

File recovery, backup restoration, and coordination with data recovery specialists for failed drives or accidentally deleted data.

Remote & On-Site Support

Most issues can be resolved remotely through secure remote access. For hardware problems or on-site requirements, we provide local San Diego support.

How Technical Support Works

Technical support is billed hourly at our standard rate. Contact us when you need help, describe the issue, and we'll work with you to resolve it efficiently. No contracts, no monthly minimums—just expert technical assistance when you need it.

For businesses that need ongoing support, our managed IT services provide comprehensive coverage with predictable monthly costs. Technical support is ideal for specific issues, project work, or businesses evaluating whether managed services make sense for them.
