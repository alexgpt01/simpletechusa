Why Businesses Choose Simple.Tech

The technology services industry has long suffered from misaligned incentives. We've built our practice on a fundamentally different model—one where your success is our singular objective.

OUR DISTINCTION

IT Services Without Hidden Conflicts

Most IT service providers earn significant revenue from vendor partnerships and product markups, creating conflicts of interest. Simple.Tech operates differently—we're a truly independent technology advisor that prioritizes your business outcomes over commission-based sales.

Zero Vendor Commissions

Traditional MSPs and IT consultants earn 20-40% margins on hardware, software licenses, and cloud services they resell. This creates pressure to recommend expensive solutions that maximize their profit.

Simple.Tech accepts absolutely no vendor kickbacks, referral fees, or reseller margins. When we recommend Microsoft 365, AWS, cybersecurity tools, or any technology, it's because it's the right fit for your business—not because we earn a commission.

Result: Technology recommendations based purely on business value

Transparent IT Billing

Our invoices contain clear line items with detailed descriptions of work performed. No surprise overtime charges, no hidden fees, no ambiguous "maintenance" costs.

Business leaders and finance teams appreciate predictable IT expenses that match quoted rates exactly. Every hour is documented and justified.

Result: Accurate IT budget forecasting and financial planning

Executive-Ready Technology Guidance

CIOs, CTOs, and business executives need IT strategy explained in business terms—ROI, risk mitigation, competitive advantage, operational efficiency.

We present technology options with clear trade-offs, cost implications, and business impact. No technical jargon that obscures critical decisions.

Result: Informed technology decisions aligned with business strategy

Senior Engineer Direct Access

When you contact Simple.Tech, you speak directly with senior systems engineers who have solved complex IT challenges across multiple industries.

No Level 1 support tickets, no waiting for escalation to "senior" staff. Issues are diagnosed and resolved on first contact by experienced professionals who understand both technology and business requirements.

Result: Faster problem resolution and minimal business disruption
